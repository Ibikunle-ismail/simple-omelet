Simple Omellet Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal. This classic omellet combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables or meats.

Preparation time
  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filings:cheese,diced vegeatables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the egg: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed . You can add a table spoon of water or milk of a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the omellete: Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the egg begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny at the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omellete
  5. Fold and serve: As the omellete continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute,then slide it into a plate
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot with additional salt and pepper if needeed
